Lecture Notes:
- Slide 13: Method of Loci is a mnemonic technique for improving memory recall.
- Method of Loci involves mentally walking along a familiar path, associating images with items or locations along the path.
- Example: Using your house as a mental path for memory recall.
- Start at the back door of your house and visualize the first item you see.
- Associate the first piece of information you want to remember with that item.
- Move on to the next location in your house, such as the kitchen.
- Visualize the first thing you see in the kitchen and associate the second item with it.
- Continue this process for other locations in your house, associating each item with a specific location.
- By mentally retracing your path, you can recall the items and the information associated with them.
